What This Is

TheVpod ni iki?

TheVpod ifata ikoranabuhanga, ubwenge bw'ubukorikori, n'ubumenyi — ibintu bifite ingaruka ku buzima bwacu bwa buri munsi — ikabisobanura mu Kinyarwanda kugira ngo abantu babyumve neza.

The world moves fast. AI is changing jobs. Science is reshaping medicine. Tech is rewriting power. Most of this conversation happens in languages that leave most of us out. TheVpod changes that.
